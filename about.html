About Us

Our mission is to provide a Classification app that would classify the uploaded data as per PSPF classification standards


In a world where data has become the most important aspect of any organisation, safeguarding the information in the data is equally immportant.

Classification of data is one of the best ways to organise and categorise data for future purposes. It also makes monitoring data easy.

As Master of Networking students we hold the priviledge of developing this web application that allows to classify the data and view detailed analysis on the go.
